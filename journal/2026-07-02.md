## What Nara is (one paragraph, for future sessions)

Tropical astrology reframed honestly as the human-subjective sky: the ecliptic as seen from Earth, anchored to equinoxes and solstices, with precession stated plainly — the tropical signs are a human frame on the year, not the constellations, and that is the point. Sibling of kali-paradevi (the objective deep sky); together they form the Trika metagrammar. ## Decisions made today
