# Nara

The human-subjective sky.

**Nara** is a study project about tropical astrology, reframed honestly as what it actually is: a human frame laid over the year. The tropical zodiac is anchored to the equinoxes and solstices — Earth's seasons, the ecliptic as seen from here — not to the distant stars. Because of the precession of the equinoxes (a slow wobble of Earth's axis, one full cycle in roughly 26,000 years), the tropical signs no longer line up with the constellations they were named after. Many treat that as an embarrassment for astrology. Nara treats it as the honest starting point: the tropical zodiac was never really about the constellations. It is about the year as lived from Earth — light returning, light leaving — and about the meaning humans have layered onto that cycle for a very long time. That makes it a fitting home for the subjective: not claims about distant objects, but a record of human meaning-making.

## The Trika architecture

Nara has a sibling project, [kali-paradevi](https://github.com/PlayfulProcess/kali-paradevi), which works the other side of the sky: the objective deep sky — black holes, galaxies, real catalog objects with measured distances. Together the two projects form a metagrammar called **Trika**:

| Project | Sky | Register |
|---|---|---|
| **nara** (this repo) | the ecliptic, the seasons, the tropical zodiac | the human-subjective sky — how the year looks and means from here |
| **kali-paradevi** | black holes, galaxies, the deep sky | the objective sky — real objects, real data, with a reflective layer on top |

The names come from the nondual Śaiva Trika triad — *nara* (the bound individual), *śakti* (power), *śiva* (consciousness) — a vocabulary the builder studies and enjoys. In that vocabulary, *nara* is the limited, embodied experient: exactly the position from which tropical astrology makes sense, since the whole system is the sky as experienced from one small place. The naming honors that relationship; neither project claims to teach or represent Śaiva Tantra, and the doctrinal grounding stays in attributed study notes.

## The humility charter

This content is produced for educational and study purposes only. We do not claim special sources, a particular tradition's authority, or any kind of special insight. The builder simply enjoys researching these subjects and spending time with them, and hopes to form a community of like-hearted, light-hearted, like-minded people who do this for the sake of the passion for truth.

No prediction, no determinism: the system never claims your birth moment was deterministic. But if you choose to hold a moment as meaningful — special enough to have left a mark worth contemplating — you can investigate it as a chart. The belief is yours to bring; the system supplies an honest sky. This is an invitation, never a claim (the non-consent axiom: nothing here may claim to know you better than you know yourself).

## What this is NOT

- **Not doctrine.** No claim to carry any tradition's authority — not Hellenistic astrology's, not Śaiva Tantra's, not anyone's. Historical sources are quoted with attribution and dates, and flagged as historical interpretation, not truth.
- **Not determinism.** No claim that the sky at your birth fixed your personality or your fate. The astronomy here is real (equinoxes, solstices, the ecliptic, precession); the meaning-making is explicitly reflective and chosen.
- **Not fortune-telling.** No predictions. A sign, a season, or a chart is an invitation to look at your own life, never a verdict about it.
- **Not sidereal astronomy dressed up.** Nara does not pretend the tropical signs are the constellations. They diverged, and the divergence is stated plainly wherever it matters.

## Platform plan (4 layers)

The same shape as the sibling projects on [recursive.eco](https://recursive.eco):

1. **Repo** (this) — the canonical open home: design docs, research notes, grammar data, journal, and the course.
2. **Grammars** — the tropical systems as recursive.eco grammars (`grammars/`): items with an honest sky layer and a clearly separated mirror layer, images from public-domain sources.
3. **Channel** — a Nara channel on recursive.eco collecting the grammars and related tools (later).
4. **Casting** — sky-maps: a time and a place resolved into what the ecliptic looked like from there, rendered as a map you can sit with (later; see `DESIGN.md`, which builds on kali-paradevi's casting design).
