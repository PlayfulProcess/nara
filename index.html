Nara

The human-subjective sky — a tropical zodiac read honestly, as a human frame laid over the year.

Nara is a study project about tropical astrology, reframed as what it actually is: a record of the year as lived from Earth. The tropical zodiac is anchored to the equinoxes and solstices — Earth's seasons — not to the distant stars; because of precession, the signs no longer line up with the constellations they were named after. Nara treats that as the honest starting point. The astronomy here is real; the meaning is ours to hold lightly. No prediction, no determinism — an invitation to look at your own life, never a verdict about it.
